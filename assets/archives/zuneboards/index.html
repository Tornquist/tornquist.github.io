[FINAL] Arcade Classics v2.75 (now w/ Frogger)
Game Name: Arcade Classics
Creator: Nathan Tornquist
Version: 2.75 Final

Important! If the back button is hit at any time, the game will exit.

Description / Instructions / Anything you want to say:
This is a collection of classic arcade games, and various older games including Frogger, Space Invaders and Snake.

Changes in 2.75:
Music Support Added
Frogger Added
Fall Added
Instructions Added for All Games

License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported

Download URL:
Arcade Classics.zip CCGame
Arcade Classics.zip Source

Modified Version (xeatmyshorts) Unsupported
Font: JuneGull


Instructions:
Solo Pong:
Try to keep the ball withing the boundaries of the screen. (The white box means nothing) The up-button moves the left paddle up and the right paddle down, the down-button moves the left paddle down and the right paddle up. The top and bottom paddles work the same way.

Snake:
Try to collect the apples and avoid the rocks. If you go off the screen you will reappear on the other side, as long as you do not hit a rock on the other side. Use the D-Pad to move.

Snake Classic:
Collect the hollow boxes. Move with the D-Pad, and stay within the screen.

Space Invaders:
Try to stay alive as long as possible. Shoot with center, move left and right with the D-Pad. You can only shoot one bullet at a time. The bases can be destroyed. They do not influence your score, but they are good places to hid behind. When the UFO comes, if you hit it you will get bonus points. You have three lives, try to get as many aliens as possible. The speed of the aliens is based on how many aliens are left. The more you kill, the faster they go. The points that you get from each alien is based on the type. The points increase the farther they are from the base.

Frogger:
Try to get your frog to the lily pads on the other side of the screen, without drowning or getting hit by a car.

Fall:
Try to avoid being pushed off the screen.
